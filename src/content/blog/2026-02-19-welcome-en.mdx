This blog is where I document how I work across product thinking, engineering delivery, and practical AI adoption.

I want the site to show not only finished pages, but also how decisions are made: what gets scoped first, what is validated early, where automation actually helps, and what changes after release.

Over time, this should become a useful layer of context around the projects on the site: not just what was shipped, but how the work was framed and why particular choices were made.

## What is coming next

- product case studies and delivery breakdowns
- notes on AI workflows, agents, and automation
- implementation write-ups on architecture, content systems, and deployment
