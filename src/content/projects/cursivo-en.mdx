Cursivo is a platform that transforms fragmented rental operations into a predictable and scalable business. We focused on making AI a practical tool for owners: from lead qualification in the funnel to tracking the ROI of every single vehicle.

## 🔴 The Problem
Without end-to-end analytics, rental businesses often run "blind":
1. **Lead Leakage**: It's unclear at which stage customers drop off or how efficiently the team is performing.
2. **Risk Assessment**: Reliable and fast customer screening is difficult to achieve manually.
3. **Hidden Costs**: Tracking the real-time profitability of individual vehicles, considering all variable costs, is a significant challenge.

## ⚡️ The Solution: Intelligent Yield Management

### 1. Sales Funnel Analytics
The system tracks the customer's journey from the first touchpoint to the closed deal. AI analyzes response times (Time-to-Reply) and identifies bottlenecks where the business is losing potential revenue.

### 2. Fleet Profitability Insights
[Placeholder for screenshot: Profitability graph for a specific vehicle]
We implemented real-time profitability tracking. The system shows the net income for each vehicle, accounting for seasonal trends and operational costs.

### 3. AI Profit Advisor
Instead of static reports, Cursivo provides actionable business insights:
- "This vehicle has been idle for over 3 days. Recommended price reduction: 10%."
- "The profitability of this model is declining. Optimal time to phase it out: next month."
- "Manager A's lead processing efficiency is 40% higher than the team average."

## ⏱ Saving Resources
We replaced bulky Excel sheets with a unified interface. The deal cycle is shortened because all critical data - from documents to scoring results - is available instantly. The team can focus on customer service rather than manual data entry.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Next.js** (App Router) - for a fast and responsive user experience.
- **Prisma + PostgreSQL** - a robust architecture for data management.
- **OpenAI API** - for lead data analysis and business insight generation.
- **Linear** - for streamlined feature management and development.
