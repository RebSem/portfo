Cursivo is a management system that turns chaotic rental operations into a predictable business.
 We focused on making AI a practical tool for owners: from the moment a lead enters the funnel to tracking the ROI of every single car.

## 🔴 The Problem
Without deep analytics, rental businesses often run "blind":
1. **Slow Leads**: It's unclear where customers drop off in the funnel or how quickly managers respond.
2. **Risk Assessment**: Reliable customer screening is difficult without slow, manual checks.
3. **Hidden Losses**: Tracking the dynamic profitability of individual vehicles, considering all costs, is a challenge.

## ⚡️ The Solution: Intelligent Yield Management

### 1. End-to-End Lead Analysis
The system tracks the customer's journey from the first touch. AI analyzes response times (Time-to-Reply) and identifies bottlenecks in the sales funnel where the business is losing money.

### 2. Fleet Dynamics and Profitability
[Placeholder for screenshot: Profitability graph for a specific vehicle]
We implemented real-time profitability tracking. The system shows exactly how much "net" profit each car brings and how that number changes based on the season and usage type.

### 3. AI Profit Advisor
Instead of just showing static charts, Cursivo provides actionable advice:
- "This vehicle has been idle for too long, consider a 10% price reduction."
- "The profitability of this model is declining — it might be time to sell it."
- "Manager A processes leads twice as fast as Manager B."

## ⏱ Saving Time (and Nerves)
We eliminated the need for manual Excel calculations. The deal cycle is shortened because all data — from documents to scoring — is ready the moment the client card is opened. Managers focus on service, not data entry.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Next.js** (App Router) — for a responsive UI.
- **Prisma + PostgreSQL** — for reliable data storage.
- **OpenAI API** — for lead data analysis and business advice generation.
- **Linear** — for managing all project features and bugs.
