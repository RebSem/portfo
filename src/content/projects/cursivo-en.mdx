# Cursivo: AI-CRM for car rental businesses

[cursivo.xyz](https://cursivo.xyz) is my own product, which I lead from product logic to UI and release. The idea is to close a specific operational pain car rental companies have: when deals and fleet economics live in Excel and WhatsApp, and decisions are made by gut feel.

## The class of problem

Car rental is an operationally heavy business — leads, documents, client screening, maintenance, downtime, seasonality. Without an end-to-end system the owner can't see:

- where leads actually drop off and which managers lose conversion;
- which clients carry anomalous risk (documents, history, behavior);
- which car is already running at a loss once downtime and service are factored in.

All three usually get patched manually or not at all. Cursivo wraps them into one workflow.

## What's inside

- **End-to-end sales funnel.** Time-to-reply, stage conversion, bottlenecks — without a separate BI tool or spreadsheet.
- **AI client and document scoring.** We structure incoming data and surface risk before the car leaves the lot.
- **Fleet unit economics.** Per-car profitability accounting for downtime, seasonality, and operating costs.
- **AI suggestions for action, not reports.** Concrete recommendations on pricing, retiring a model, or rebalancing manager workload.

## Stack

Next.js (App Router), TypeScript, Prisma + PostgreSQL, OpenAI API, Linear for backlog management.

I can build a product end-to-end: problem framing, data model, AI layer, interface, launch. This isn't a "vibe-coded" demo — it's a working system used by real customers, not portfolio decoration.
